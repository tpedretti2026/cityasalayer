# Guidelines, Procedures & Best Practices

## Overview
This atlas documents guidelines, procedures, and best practices designed to support municipalities in managing the legacy of participatory processes on urban transformation.

### Best Practices
- Case studies of successful implementation
- Lessons learned from participatory processes

- Frameworks for sustainable engagement

### Training Modules
- Municipal staff capacity building
- Community facilitator training
- Documentation and archiving skills

## How to Use This Atlas
Each section provides practical tools that municipalities can adapt to their local context, capacity, and governance structures.
